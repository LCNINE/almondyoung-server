## Design Specifications

### Colors
- **Title Text**: Black (#000000)
- **Section Headers**: Black (#000000)
- **Body Text**: Gray (#666666 or #999999)
- **Separator Lines**: Dark gray/black (#2D2D2D)
- **Background**: White (#FFFFFF)

### Typography

**Page Title**
- **Font Weight**: Bold (700)
- **Font Size**: 48-56px
- **Line Height**: 1.2
- **Letter Spacing**: -0.5px

**Section Titles (H2)**
- **Font Weight**: Bold (700)
- **Font Size**: 32-36px
- **Line Height**: 1.3
- **Margin Bottom**: 16px

**Subsection Titles (H3)**
- **Font Weight**: Bold (700)
- **Font Size**: 24-28px
- **Line Height**: 1.4
- **Margin Bottom**: 12px

**Body Text**
- **Font Weight**: Regular (400)
- **Font Size**: 16-18px
- **Line Height**: 1.6
- **Color**: Gray

**Category List**
- **Font Weight**: Regular (400)
- **Font Size**: 16-18px
- **Line Height**: 1.6
- **Color**: Gray
- **Separator**: " / " (space-slash-space)

### Layout Structure
- **Page Width**: Full width or max-width container
- **Padding**: 40-60px on sides
- **Vertical Spacing**: 60-80px between major sections
- **Section Spacing**: 40px between subsections

### Separator Lines
- **Thickness**: 2-3px
- **Color**: Dark gray or black
- **Margin**: 20px above and below page title
- **Width**: Full width

### Content Hierarchy

1. **Level 1**: Page title (고객조회) - Largest, bold
2. **Level 2**: Main section (구매 고객정보) - Large, bold
3. **Level 3**: Subsections (종목 분류, 설명, etc.) - Medium, bold
4. **Level 4**: Body text - Regular, gray

### Spacing

**Vertical Spacing**
- **After page title**: 60px
- **Between sections**: 48px
- **After section headers**: 16px
- **Between paragraphs**: 8-12px
- **Between subsections**: 32px

**Horizontal Spacing**
- **Page margins**: 40-60px
- **Content max-width**: 800-1000px
- **Text indent**: None (left-aligned)

### Text Alignment
- **All text**: Left-aligned
- **Page title**: Left-aligned (could be centered in some designs)
- **Categories**: Left-aligned with slash separators

### Visual Style
- **Minimalist design**: Clean, simple, text-focused
- **High contrast**: Black titles on white background
- **Gray body text**: For visual hierarchy
- **Generous spacing**: Improves readability

### Responsive Considerations
- **Mobile**: Stack all content vertically
- **Tablet**: Same layout, adjust padding
- **Desktop**: Center content with max-width
- **Font scaling**: Proportional size reduction on smaller screens

### Content Structure

**Information Architecture**
1. **Title**: What is this feature
2. **Main Description**: What it does (구매 고객정보)
3. **Details**: Explanation paragraphs
4. **Categories**: Available classifications
5. **Features**: Individual feature descriptions
